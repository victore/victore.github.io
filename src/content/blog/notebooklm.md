  I don't have time, like everyone else. When I find something interesting to know about it and it's a 2 or 3 hrs talk on youtube, or when it's a blog post ranting about why AI is bad or how it won't replace us, I regret to read all that text, instead just copy the text then I go to my NotebookLM dashboard and with a pre refined prompt I create a short summary. This works well when we have no time. Later, when something in that audio becomes interesting to me then I find the time to listen the conversation.
